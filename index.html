About Me

My name is Christopher Gerard Lao,
I am a Medical Biology student that wants to change into another field, specifically IT.

You might be asking why I switched to an entirely different field, because I do not feel like I am suited for the intense pressure of the medical field, which is why I left the medical field before I start to waver my dedication to helping people medically. I have been having difficulties in studying and with the lack of support from anyone in the world nowadays it is hard to continue studying at all unless you have incredibly high dedication and motivation. The environment for studying is incredibly toxic, that a student who also has a hard time may just be left behind. Furthermore, the way of education is incredibly outdated, and the way of teaching has not changed for the better, it might even have changed for the worse.

GOALS

Which is why I wanted to create a way for anyone to learn and not just to study. Something that can make learning visible to ones one progress, so that people dont give up immediately because they dont see any progress. I wanted to create a system to see people's progress as well as a way to personalize the learning experience, this way we can fully utilize the strengths of each individual and help them.

But why IT then, why not go to an education field? Because I believe I am able to learn how to help people by myself, but I am not able to learn how to create a system to help people by myself. I will need the help of mentors who are willing to support me despite the dryness of empathy in not only my university bu also in the world.
